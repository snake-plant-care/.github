The Ultimate Guide to Snake Plant Care

How to Care for Your Snake Plant

Snake plants, also called Sansevieria, are perfect for beginners who want a low-maintenance, air-purifying houseplant. These hardy plants thrive with minimal care, but there are a few key tips to keep in mind.

Snake plants love bright, indirect sunlight but can survive in low light too. Overwatering is their biggest enemy—water them only when the soil is completely dry.
